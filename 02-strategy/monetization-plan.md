## 1. Monetization Philosophy

**Core principle:** The free tier must be genuinely useful — good enough that users love the app and tell their friends. Premium unlocks depth and power, not basic functionality.

**Why this matters:** Car enthusiasts are passionate but price-sensitive in Bulgaria. A crippled free tier creates resentment. A generous free tier creates evangelists who convert to premium when they want more.

**No ads.** The app should feel premium from day one. Ads undermine the "intelligence platform" positioning and annoy the exact users we need to retain. Revenue comes from value, not attention.

## 2. Pricing Structure

### Premium Subscription

| Plan | Price | Price in лв (approx.) | Discount |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly | €2.99/month | ~5.85 лв/month | — |
| Annual | €24.99/year | ~48.85 лв/year | 30% vs monthly |

**Why €2.99/month:**
- Competitors charge €5-15/year (lifetime or annual). €2.99/month is significantly higher, but we offer significantly more value (intelligence, not just logging).
- In Bulgarian context: €2.99/month is roughly the price of one car wash. For someone spending €200-500/month on their car, it's a rounding error if the value is clear.
- The annual plan at €24.99 (~€2.08/month) provides a strong conversion incentive and reduces churn.
- Low enough to be an impulse decision, high enough to generate meaningful revenue at scale.

**Pricing for expansion markets (future):**
| Market | Monthly | Annual | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bulgaria (launch) | €2.99 | €24.99 | Local purchasing power |
| Romania | €2.99 | €24.99 | Similar purchasing power |
| Western Europe | €4.99 | €39.99 | Higher purchasing power, higher ARPU |

## 3. Free vs Premium Feature Split

The split follows one rule: **free gives you awareness, premium gives you intelligence.**

### Free Tier — "Know Your Costs"

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Expense tracking | All categories, unlimited entries |
| Cost dashboard | Monthly total, basic category breakdown |
| Vehicle profile | Add up to 2 vehicles |
| Vehicle timeline | Chronological history, up to 5 photos total |
| Maintenance reminders | Up to 3 active reminders |
| Fuel tracking | Fuel logging with basic consumption calculation (L/100km per fill-up) |
| Basic challenges | View leaderboards (can't compete) |

**Why this is generous:** A user can fully track their expenses and see their monthly total for free. This is already more than most people do. It solves the basic problem.

### Premium Tier — "Understand Your Costs"

| Feature | Details | Why It's Worth Paying For |
|---|---|---|
| **Cost per kilometer** | Full cost/km calculation with trends | The core intelligence metric — changes decisions |
| **Advanced analytics** | Year-over-year trends, category trends, monthly comparisons, spending forecasts | Depth that turns data into insight |
| **Model benchmarks** | "Your car costs X% more/less than average" | Unique — nobody else offers this |
| **Fuel consumption trends** | Consumption trends over time, efficiency analytics, comparison with other owners of the same model | Enthusiasts obsess over this |
| **Unlimited vehicles** | No cap on number of vehicles | Multi-car enthusiasts need this |
| **Unlimited photos** | No cap on timeline photos | Document every detail of the build |
| **Unlimited reminders** | No cap on maintenance reminders | Peace of mind for complex vehicles |
| **Export reports** | PDF service history, CSV data export | Useful for selling car, tax purposes |
| **Participate in challenges** | Compete on leaderboards, earn badges | The fun social layer |
| **Document storage** | Insurance, registration, inspection photos | Everything in one place |
| **Keep vs sell insights** | Cost trajectory analysis, maintenance trend warnings | High-value decision support |
| **Priority support** | Faster response times | Nice-to-have |

### The Paywall Trigger

The critical moment: **the user sees their monthly total (free) and wants to know their cost per kilometer (premium).** The dashboard shows "Your car costs 847 лв this month" with a locked section below: "Unlock your cost per km, trends, and how you compare to other owners → Go Premium."

This is a natural value ladder — awareness is free, intelligence is premium.

| Stream | Model | Expected Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| **Premium subscriptions** | €2.99/month or €24.99/year | Primary revenue stream |
| | | Conservative: €500-2,000/month by Month 12 |

**No other revenue streams in Phase 1.** Focus entirely on proving the product works and people will pay.

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 12-24)

| Stream | Model | Expected Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Premium subscriptions | Growing from Phase 1 | €2,000-8,000/month |
| **Affiliate partnerships** | Commission on insurance, tires, parts, mechanic referrals | €500-2,000/month |
| **Garage subscriptions** | €30-50/month per garage | €500-3,000/month |

**Affiliate approach:** Non-intrusive, contextual recommendations. Example: when a user logs a tire expense, suggest "Compare tire prices from our partners" with an affiliate link. Never pushy. Always relevant. User gets value (cheaper price), you get commission (€5-20 per conversion).

## 6. Free-to-Premium Conversion Strategy

### Soft Paywall Triggers

These are moments where the user naturally encounters premium value:

| Trigger Moment | What Happens | Premium Tease |
|---|---|---|
| User views dashboard | Monthly total is visible (free) | "Unlock cost per km →" locked section below |
| User logs 10th expense | Pattern is established | "See how your costs compare to other [car model] owners" |
| User adds 3rd vehicle | Hits free limit | "Unlock unlimited vehicles with Premium" |
| User uploads 6th photo | Hits free limit | "Unlimited photos with Premium" |
| User views leaderboard | Can see rankings | "Go Premium to compete and earn badges" |
| User's car hits a cost milestone | E.g., 10,000 лв total | "Get predictive insights: what will your car cost next year?" |
| 30 days after signup | Established habit | In-app message: "You've tracked X лв in expenses. See your full intelligence dashboard →" |

### What NOT to Do

- Never lock basic expense logging — this kills retention before conversion can happen
- Never show intrusive full-screen paywalls on app open
- Never limit the number of expenses (this punishes active users)
- Never make the free app feel broken or crippled
- Never do a free trial that converts to paid automatically (trust killer in Bulgarian market)

### Conversion Psychology

The progression should feel natural:
1. **Week 1:** User logs expenses, sees monthly total → "This is useful"
2. **Week 2-3:** User builds a habit, timeline grows → "I like documenting my car"
3. **Week 4:** User wants deeper insights, sees locked features → "I wonder what my cost per km is"
4. **Month 2:** User is invested in the data → "€2.99 is nothing compared to what I spend on my car"

## 7. Pricing Experiments to Run Post-Launch

| Experiment | What to Test | How |
|---|---|---|
| Price sensitivity | €1.99 vs €2.99 vs €3.99 monthly | A/B test with different user cohorts |
| Annual vs monthly | Emphasize annual discount more/less | Test paywall design variations |
| Feature gating | Which locked feature drives most conversions? | Track which paywall trigger has highest conversion |
| Free trial | 7-day premium trial vs direct paywall | A/B test conversion rates and retention |
| Lifetime deal | One-time €49.99 payment option | Test in a limited campaign |

## 9. Revenue Risk Assessment

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Premium conversion below 5% | 🔴 High | Experiment with pricing, feature gating, and free trial. If persistent, accelerate B2B revenue. |
| Bulgarian market too small for meaningful B2C revenue | 🟡 Medium | Plan expansion to Romania by Month 9-12. Don't wait for Year 2. |
| Users churn after 1 month of premium | 🟡 Medium | Monthly value reinforcement: "This month Premium saved you..." insights. Annual plan reduces churn mechanically. |
| Competitors copy the intelligence features | 🟢 Low | Community data moat grows over time. Move fast on benchmarks and challenges. |
| B2B modules take longer to build than expected | 🟡 Medium | Keep B2C subscription as primary revenue. B2B is upside, not survival. |
| Apple/Google take 30% of subscription revenue | 🔴 High (structural) | Factor 70% net into all projections. Consider web-based premium purchase to avoid store fees (risky with store policies). |

## 10. Key Decisions

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Ads | No ads | Preserves premium feel, aligns with intelligence positioning |
| Price | €2.99/month, €24.99/year | Balances accessibility with revenue per user |
| Free tier | Generous — full expense tracking, limited analytics | Builds habit and evangelism before conversion |
| Paywall style | Soft, contextual — never blocks core functionality | Respects the user, converts through demonstrated value |
| B2B timing | Phase 2 (after 5,000+ active users) | B2C user base creates leverage for B2B sales |
| Affiliates | Phase 2 (not MVP) | Focus on product-market fit first |
